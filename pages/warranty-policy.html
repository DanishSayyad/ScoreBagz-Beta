Warranty Policy

Quality products, backed by our commitment

Thank you for choosing our product. We take pride in the quality of our bags and aim to provide the best service to our customers. Please read the following Warranty Policy carefully to understand the terms and conditions of service and replacement.

Warranty Coverage

When We Provide Service:

1

Within Warranty Period

The bag is still covered under the valid warranty period from the date of purchase.

2

Zip or Runner Issues

If the zipper or runner of the bag is not working properly.

3

Stitching Problems

If the stitching of the bag comes out or becomes loose.

4

Replacement Cases

If the issue is major, hard to repair, or beyond service limits, the company may replace the bag with a new one of similar value and model (subject to availability).

5

Warranty Proof Required

When you come for bag service, you must bring the Warranty Card and one proof of purchase (such as a bill or receipt). If you do not have these documents, you must provide your registered phone number for verification.

Warranty Exclusions

When We Do NOT Provide Service:

6

Out of Warranty Period

If the bag is no longer within the warranty or service period.

7

Fabric or Material Damage

Damage to the bag's fabric, material, or cloth is not covered under this warranty.

8

Color Fading or Shading

Any color fading or shading due to sunlight, washing, or regular use is not considered a defect.

9

Misuse or Overloading

Damage caused by misuse, such as carrying more than the recommended weight limit (e.g., using the bag for loads above 500g when the limit is 500g), is not covered. The company shall not be responsible for such misuse.

10

Manufacturing Defects Beyond Repair

If the bag has a manufacturing defect that cannot be repaired, replacement will be handled as per company policy. However, if the damage is due to customer misuse or external causes, service will not be provided.

11

Damage During Delivery or Natural Calamities

The warranty does not cover any damage caused during delivery, transportation, or due to natural events such as floods, fire, earthquakes, or other acts of nature.

Additional Terms

  • Warranty applies only to the original purchaser and is not transferable.
  • The company reserves the right to decide whether a bag will be repaired or replaced based on the type and extent of the defect.
  • This warranty does not cover any indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
  • The company's decision on all warranty claims shall be final and binding.
